What is Solved?

We know that Chinese characters can seem daunting, and that rote memorization can be painful, so we created Solved: an easy, fun, authentic, and culturally meaningful way to learn Chinese characters. 

How? Chinese characters are actually made from a small base of pictographic building-blocks (called “radicals”). They are like roots and affixes in English yet even easier to remember because they generally look like what they represent. Solved teaches you these building-blocks and how they piece together. You will find that not only Chinese characters “make sense” now, you could even speculate the meaning and pronunciation of a character that you’ve never learned! This method, formally called “radical breakdown,” is how native speakers learn Chinese characters. It has been proven effective both theoretically and experimentally by many studies.

Solved incorporates traditional insight with modern technology. It is powered by research-based and individualized learning strategies. Read more about them here.

Solved is committed to being completely, forever free.
